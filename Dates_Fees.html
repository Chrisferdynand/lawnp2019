Important dates and fee payment

Dates

Please, send proposals to lawnp2019@fiumsa.edu.bo with "Mini-symposium Proposal" in the subject of the e-mail. Include title, organizer, summary (1000 characters maximum), speakers and speaker affiliation. Minimum of speakers for mini-symposia: 4

Fee payment

Take in mind that fees are reduced if you pay before September 21nd, 2019.

The fees will cover participation in the conference, the welcome reception (Monday, October 21st, 2019), conference materials, coffee breaks, banquet and cultural event, and touristic tour. There will be prizes for the best posters.

The way to pay the fees will be added here as soon as possible. Thank you for your patience.

Lawnp 2019

LAWNP have as target to create a motivating environment, such that researchers from Latin America and from other parts of the world can present and discuss not only their own latest results but also the trends and perspectives in this very interdisciplinary field of investigation.
